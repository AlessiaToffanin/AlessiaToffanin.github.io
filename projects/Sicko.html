// Sicko is a project that takes a speculative approach: What would happen if the stigma associated with the outburst of anger vanished? What if it lost its negative connotation and became a means of social comparison? Sicko is a project that revolves around the user's experience— from the initial interaction with the brand to the use of devices, and ultimately, the application of gamification on the digital platform. The playful aspect, coupled with a sarcastic and provocative tone, is designed to challenge users, promote competitive behavior, and encourage the release of their suppressed anger.
