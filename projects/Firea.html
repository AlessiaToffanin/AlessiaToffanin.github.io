// Firea is a service product system consisting of a candle stove and a beehive adoption service. The goal is to make beeswax a new source of cheap and sustainable energy, allowing people to have a source of heating that is simpler, sustainable and supportive of small local producers. Firea encourages user engagement, with the ultimate goal of raising awareness of natural raw materials and disseminating information about their potential applications.
